// my story.

1990

I was born on May 11, in Turin, and I am the first daughter of my wonderful family. My parents met kayaking and passed on to me their passion for nature and adventures, although I was born in the city I have always preferred green and wilder places.
This is me and my mommy.

2004

After finishing middle school, my family decided to move to Tuscany, to a small town where my brother and I could grow up with more freedom (and less worry for them). It was difficult at first but I am grateful for their choice.

2009

Despite the turbulence of my high school and teenage years, I finished my studies in the right time and after graduating I started working, gaining various experiences and focusing on restoration services.
This is me and my daddy when we went to Rome after my degree.

2013

At some point I decided to turn all the tables (typical of late adolescence) and left as an au pair, destination Finland. I had the great fortune to meet a wonderful family that hosted and supported me, as well as my own. The plan was to stay, but the cold and homesickness were too much and I returned home.

2016

After a short break at home, I decided to move permanently to Turin (...again) because I would have more opportunities to work and grow professionally in a big city rather than in a small town. I also started university and that is where my love for web development was born.

2019

The Covid and lock-down years were decisive in solidifying my passion for web development. I took and completed several related courses, and I began to realize that that was the path I wanted to take seriously to turn my life around.

2023

And here I am, more determined than ever to pursue my life project, the one that will change my fortunes forever: to become a web developer.
